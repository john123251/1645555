Genesis period represent the initial supply of SPACE for the successful launch of Space Finance. Initial mint of 500,000 SPACE will be distributed in 24h period by 2 single asset staking pools - WETH and DAI.

This is done to protect the best interest of our investors and SPACE token's peg price. Additionally, we believe this will decrease the likelihood of whales dumping SPACE during the Genesis Pool

Space Finance's PEG Protection is what makes it unique. By imposing a 5% fee on sales of $SPACE in exchanges when PEG is lost, we are able to provide stability for the token. All fees are burned, which reduces total circulating supply and helps boost it back to PEG. When we are above 1 PEG, there are no fees for selling!
